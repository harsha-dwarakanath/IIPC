InnoForge: Ideate & Prototype Club's

  • IDEATION SPARK
  • COLLABORATIVE SYNERGY
  • CELEBRATING EXCELLENCE

InnoForge cultivates an environment that encourages students and faculty to unleash their creativity and generate novel ideas, addressing real-world problems and challenges.

Prototyping Playground

This hub provides opportunities for students and faculty to learn and apply prototyping techniques, tools, and technologies, transforming their ideas into tangible prototypes.

Skill Enrichment

Through workshops, seminars, and skill-building sessions, InnoForge enhances students' and faculty's knowledge in areas such as design thinking, rapid prototyping, and project management.

InnoForge fosters a collaborative environment where students and faculty from various disciplines can converge, share their expertise, and contribute to each other's projects.

Cross-Pollination of Ideas

This melting pot of diverse perspectives encourages the cross-pollination of ideas, fostering interdisciplinary collaboration and collective problem-solving.

Knowledge Exchange

The free exchange of knowledge and expertise creates a fertile ground for personal and professional growth, enabling participants to learn from one another.

InnoForge provides prestigious platforms for students and faculty to showcase their prototypes and earn well-deserved recognition through renowned competitions and award ceremonies.

Spotlight on Innovation

These events celebrate innovation, highlighting the potential of students' and faculty's ideas to make a meaningful impact on the world.

Inspiring Future Innovators

By showcasing outstanding prototypes, InnoForge aims to inspire and motivate the next generation of innovators, fostering a culture of creativity and problem-solving.
