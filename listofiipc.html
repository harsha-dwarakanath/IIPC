List of IIPC Activities

Event Summary

Smart Idea Challenge- 2023

Smart Idea Challenge- 2023 is conducted for RVRJCCE students during 15th and 16th September, 2023.

Smart India Hackathon 2023

20 ideas were submitted to Smart India Hackathon 2023.

Smart India Hackathon (SIH) is an annual event organized by the Government of India to encourage innovation and problem-solving through technology. Two teams (each of 6 students) participated in the grand finale from our college during 19-23, December, 2023. The teams were presented their prototypes at respective nodal centers: “Psycheck”-S/w edition- Manipal University, Jaipur, Rajasthan; “Aqua Therapy”- h/w edition- Bhilai Institute of Technology, Durg, Chattisgarh.

Prototyping Competition

Prototyping competition is held for RVRJCCE students with an aim to provide funding support for top prototypes on November 18, 2023 and four prototypes are selected for funding support. College Management has sanctioned total Rs. 2,98,450/- for top 4 prototypes in the competition.

National Level Hackathon “Vudhvikash”

A National level Hackathon “Vudhvikash” is organized on December 1st & 2nd 2023.

Innovation, Design, and Entrepreneurship (IDE) Bootcamp

One team was selected and participated in “Innovation, Design, and Entrepreneurship (IDE) Bootcamp” organized by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and the Ministry of Education's Innovation Cell (MIC) at Vardhaman College of Engineering, Hyderabad during 29th January to 2nd February 2024.
